If you have signed up for a training event, please do be sure to attend, or to let us know that you can't make it. Just not turning up wastes a training slot, and training slots are usually a limiting resource.

The system records no-shows, and if you do it more than a set number of times (currently 2) it will block you from signing up for more training, until you have seen one of the management team to explain and to give convincing assurance that you won't do it again.
