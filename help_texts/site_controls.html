This section sets your site controls, including privacy.

As your relationship with equipment types is defined by you having completed the relevent training event, in the context of training "host" means the trainer and "attendee" the person being trained. If you're hosting an event / training people, it would be helpful for your name to be visible to attendees / trainees, unless you are extremely concerned for your privacy. Likewise, but not quite as strongly so, it would be good for hosts of events to be able to see who will be attending the events.

General visibility is probably good for the community as a whole, but less important than those two specific forms.
