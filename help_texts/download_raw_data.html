This is an admin / developer operation, that will retrieve a collection from the mongo databases underlying the system.

This performs little or no interpretation of the data. In particular, neither the person data nor the equipment type data contains a list of the equipment a person is qualified on or the people who are qualified on a type of equipment; this information is represented in the events collection, and constructed for use from what training events a person has successfully attended.

Note that the "profiles" collection is confidential information under the GDPR.
