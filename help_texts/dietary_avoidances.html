The dietary avoidances section is used when preparing for an event at which food will be provided. The event hosts' view of the event page will show counts for all the combinations of dietary avoidances of people who have signed up for the meeting. The choices of individuals will not be shown.

If you don't indicate what you don't eat, you are at risk of being counted in for peperoni pizza or similar.

Although the choices of individuals will not be shown, if you are the only attendee, it may be possible to deduce them, and as some dietary avoidances link to religious observance, and that is something for which people are sometimes persecuted, this should be regarded as a possibly sensitive issue for privacy.
